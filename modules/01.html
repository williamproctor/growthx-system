Key Takeaways

Core principles

  • Your buyer's first "sales call" now happens inside an AI answer — if you're not in it, you don't exist.
  • AI-referred traffic converts 3-5× higher than brand search. The game changed, but the math got better.
  • The system teaches thinking, not tools. Mental models, frameworks, and first principles compound; tools expire.
  • Three audiences now: buyers, AI engines, and training bots. Your strategy must serve all three.
  • Workflow before technology — understand the discipline before buying the dashboard.
