Module 01

The 15-Minute System Overview

How AI rewired the way buyers find and trust your company, and the model that sits underneath every module in this system.

~15 min
3 video clips

Marcel is recording a dedicated 15-minute system overview. In the meantime, these clips cover the core concepts from existing workshop recordings.

Right now — while you're reading this — your buyer is having a sales call without you. They opened ChatGPT, or Claude, or Perplexity, and typed something like "best platforms for [your category] at a Series B company." They're getting personalized recommendations. Expert-level analysis. A ready-made shortlist. The whole conversation takes 15–20 minutes, and you're not in the room.

This isn't a hypothetical. Gartner's latest data says 94% of B2B buyers now use LLMs somewhere in their buying process. Ninety-four percent. That's not early-adopter territory. It's default behavior. And if your company isn't showing up inside those AI answers, you don't exist for a growing slice of your market.

Clip 01 The Buyer Behavior Shift — 3 min
Why AI has changed how buyers discover, evaluate, and trust content.

That's why we built this system. It's not a tool demo or a prompt library. It's a way of thinking. These frameworks come from 15+ years building growth engines at HashiCorp, ServiceTitan, Scale AI, and Deepgram, places where budgets demanded results and nobody cared about your tech stack. Tools change every six months. The thinking behind them compounds for decades.

Here's the engine underneath everything we do: Content earns attention → Quality builds trust → Trust drives distribution → Distribution drives growth. Each layer feeds the next. Skip one and it stalls. Get all four working together and the whole thing accelerates, with each cycle making the next one cheaper and faster.

Clip 02 The Value-First Philosophy — 4 min
Value-first, iterate fast, build in public, compound via systems.

Now the math that trips people up. "If AI gives the answer directly, nobody clicks through to my site, so what's the point?" Fair question. Click-through rates from AI answers are lower than traditional search. Maybe 70% of clicks disappear. But AI-referred traffic converts 3–5x higher than brand search. The people who do click through already trust you. They've been pre-sold by the AI's recommendation. Fewer clicks, but each one is worth a lot more.

Clip 03 The Sphere of Influence Model — 3 min
Concentric layers: core → platforms → reputation — what you can control.

This module is the map. We start here so you can see the whole picture: where AI visibility fits, why it matters, and how the pieces connect. After this, assess your gaps. Then go straight to the modules that matter most for your situation. You don't have to do all of it, just the right parts, in the right order.

Key Takeaways

  • Your buyer's first "sales call" now happens inside an AI answer. If you're not in it, you don't exist.
  • AI-referred traffic converts 3–5x higher than brand search. Fewer clicks, but each one is worth more.
  • This system teaches thinking, not tools. Tools expire. The mental models behind them don't.
  • You're now writing for three audiences: buyers, AI engines, and training crawlers. Your strategy has to work for all of them.
  • Workflow before technology. Learn the discipline before you buy the dashboard.
