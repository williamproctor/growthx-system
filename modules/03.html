Key Takeaways

Core principles

  • Start with your audience, not keywords. Understanding daily reality, pain points, and jobs-to-be-done comes first.
  • LLMs reward four things: well-researched/attributed, comprehensive, brand-reputable, and fresh content.
  • Content is becoming "long-tail and personal" — buyers now type detailed, contextual queries into AI.
  • Artifacts (company context, audience personas, writing guidelines) are living documents that serve as shared truth.
  • Content optimized for genuine human value is what ultimately gets preferred by AI models too.
