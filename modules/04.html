Key Takeaways

Core principles

  • One-shot prompts produce 40-50% quality at best. Context artifacts are what close the gap.
  • The quality bar: credible, comprehensive, and fresh. Every process should serve these three pillars.
  • Context beats prompt engineering — always invest in the inputs, not the instructions.
  • "If one-click generation is easy for you, a thousand competitors are pressing that button too."
  • Expert-in-the-loop means experts set context, shape strategy, and make judgment calls — not write from scratch.
