Key Takeaways

Core principles

  • 60-82% of AI training data comes from crawled web pages. Your website is the atomic unit of AI visibility.
  • Content older than 30 days gets 3.2× fewer citations. Freshness is a lever, not a nice-to-have.
  • Over 50% of companies have technical issues blocking AI crawlers they don't even know about.
  • Schema markup, structured data, and technical SEO fundamentals matter more in the AI era, not less.
  • The visibility flywheel: quality content → gets crawled → gets cited → builds authority → compounds.
