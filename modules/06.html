Key Takeaways

What sticks

  • Prompts are starting points, not endpoints. Context beats prompt engineering — always.
  • Three foundational artifacts: company context, audience personas, and writing guidelines.
  • The Shaping Loop (Flip / Spread / Articulate / Chisel / Re-Anchor) teaches the meta-skill of iterating AI outputs.
  • Artifacts are living documents that improve through use — like cursor rules, they evolve with every iteration.
  • Build personas from daily reality outward, not demographics inward.
